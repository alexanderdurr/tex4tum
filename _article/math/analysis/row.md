## Arithmetic Sum
$$\sum \limits_{k=1}^{n} k = \frac{n (n+1)}{2}$$
with $k,n ∈ ℕ$

## Geometric Sum
$$\sum \limits_{k=0}^{n} q^k = \frac{1 - q^{n+1}}{1-q}$$
with $k,n ∈ ℕ$ and $q ∈ ℤ$


## Exponential row
$$\sum\limits_{n = 0}^{\infty} \frac{\cx z^n}{n!} = e^{\cx z}$$
with $n ∈ ℕ$ and $\cx z ∈ ℂ$

The n-th moment of a continuous function $f(x)$ of a real variable $x$ about a value $c$ is

$$\mu _{n}=\int _{-\infty }^{\infty }(x-c)^{n}\,f(x)\diff x$$

## Mean

discrete:
$$\mu_1 =\sum x \cdot P(x)$$


## Variance

discrete:
$$\mu_1 =\sum x \cdot P(x)$$
